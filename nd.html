Neer Dose


Ingredients:

  1. Raw rice – 1 cup
  2. Water – 2.5 to 3 cups (adjust for consistency)
  3. Salt – to taste
  4. Oil – for greasing the pan

Instructions:

  1. Wash the raw rice thoroughly until the water runs clear.Soak the rice in enough water for 4–6 hours or overnight.
  2. Drain the soaked rice and add it to a blender.
  3. Add a small amount of water and grind to a smooth paste
  4. Transfer the paste to a mixing bowl.
  5. Add more water to make the batter thin and watery, like buttermilk.
  6. Add salt to the batter and mix well.
  7. Let it rest
