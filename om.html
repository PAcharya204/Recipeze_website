Omlette


Ingredients:

  1. eggs
  2. 2 tbsp milk (optional, for a fluffy texture)
  3. Salt to taste
  4. Black pepper to taste
  5. 1 tbsp butter or oil
  6. 2 tbsp chopped onions
  7. 2 tbsp chopped tomatoes
  8. 2 tbsp chopped bell peppers (optional)
  9. 1 tbsp chopped cilantro (optional)
  10. 2 tbsp grated cheese (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, crack the eggs and add salt, black pepper, and milk (if using).
  2. Whisk well until the mixture is smooth and slightly frothy.
  3. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat.
  4. Add butter or oil and let it melt.
  5. Add chopped onions, tomatoes, and bell peppers
  6. Sauté for a minute until slightly soft.
  7. Pour the egg mixture into the pan, ensuring it spreads evenly. Tilt the pan slightly to distribute the eggs. Let it cook for 2–3 minutes until the edges start to lift.
  8. Sprinkle grated cheese on one side of the omelet.
  9. Carefully fold the omelet in half using a spatula. Cook for another 30 seconds until the cheese melts. Transfer to a plate and enjoy hot!
